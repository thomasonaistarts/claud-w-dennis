Who we are

A neighbourhood institution, one cup at a time.

Claud W Dennis has been a beloved fixture in Southgate since the beginning. What started as a passion for great coffee became a proper local haunt — the kind of place where regulars have their order memorised and strangers become friends.

We serve speciality coffee, freshly made food, and the occasional craft beer. We have iPads for those who need them, a no-laptop rule for those who don't, and a fireplace in winter that makes everything better.

The owner is almost always around. That's the point.

"Best coffee in the area. Full stop."

Coffee & Menu

Something for every hour of the day.

Coffee

Monmouth Roasted

We use Monmouth Coffee beans — one of London's most respected roasters. From a classic espresso to a flat white, cortado, filter, or cold brew, every cup is made with care. All the world's coffees, done properly.

  • Espresso & all espresso drinks
  • Filter & cold brew
  • Oat, almond & soy available
Food

Handmade & Fresh

Everything on the food menu is made by hand. Sandwiches built to order, pastries baked fresh, homemade cookies, and a rotating selection of sweet treats. Come early — the good stuff goes fast.

  • Handmade sandwiches
  • Pastries & baked goods
  • Cookies & sweet treats
Drinks

Cocktails, Beer & Wine

When the afternoon calls for something stronger, we've got you covered. Craft beers, a curated wine list, and cocktails — the kind of place you come for coffee and stay for a glass.

  • Handcrafted cocktails
  • Craft beers on rotation
  • Wine — red, white & rosé
